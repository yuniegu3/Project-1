Why Choose Munder Difflin Paper?

Our company has spent years developing the most qualified paper in the world and we are proud to announce that it is finally complete. Munder Difflin papers are 100% jam free so there is no more need of wrestling your printer to spit that report you have been trying to print for hours. Munder Difflin Paper is great for both offices or houses. Our papers are above the standard 80gsm, weighting in at 110gsm. The extra weight insures that our papers never bleed through so it makes great drawing paper for children without the worry of messy ink everywhere.
